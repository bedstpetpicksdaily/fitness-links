Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between microfiber and cotton yoga towels?

Microfiber towels are more absorbent, quick-drying, and typically feature non-slip surfaces. Cotton towels are more durable long-term but heavier and slower to dry.

Can I use a yoga mat towel on any mat?

Yes, yoga mat towels work with standard yoga mats and fit most sizes. Most are designed to cover your mat completely or partially, depending on preference.

How often should I wash my microfiber yoga towel?

Wash after every 2-3 uses or whenever you notice sweat buildup. Use cold water and skip fabric softener to preserve the non-slip grip coating.

Are microfiber yoga towels eco-friendly?

Microfiber is synthetic, but it's durable and long-lasting, reducing replacement frequency. Look for brands using sustainable manufacturing practices.
