Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between cork and foam yoga blocks?

Cork blocks are firmer, more durable, and provide excellent stability but are heavier. Foam blocks are lighter, softer, and more affordable but compress over time. Choose cork for long-term use and foam for portability.

How many yoga blocks do I need?

Most practitioners benefit from a set of 2 blocks. Some advanced poses may require 3 blocks, but a pair is sufficient for home gym use with yoga mats and other accessories.

Can yoga blocks help with home workouts?

Yes, yoga blocks enhance stretching, flexibility training, and recovery work. They pair well with resistance bands and dumbbells to create a comprehensive home gym setup.

Are yoga blocks worth the investment?

Absolutely. Quality yoga blocks support proper alignment, prevent injury, and extend the lifespan of your practice. They're affordable additions to any home gym equipment collection.
