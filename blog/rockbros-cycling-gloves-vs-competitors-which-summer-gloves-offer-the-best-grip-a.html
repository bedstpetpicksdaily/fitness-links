Are Rockbros cycling gloves suitable for hot weather riding?

Absolutely. Rockbros specifically designs their summer gloves with breathable mesh panels and moisture-wicking materials that keep hands cool and dry during hot-weather rides. The ventilation is excellent while still maintaining the grip and support needed for safe cycling.

How do Rockbros gloves compare to Pearl Izumi in terms of durability?

Rockbros gloves offer comparable durability to Pearl Izumi at a lower price point. The reinforced stitching and quality materials mean they withstand regular use and multiple washes without degrading. Many cyclists find the value proposition far superior.

What size should I choose for Rockbros cycling gloves?

Rockbros offers a comprehensive size range. Measure your hand circumference and refer to their sizing chart. Gloves should fit snugly without being restrictive—you should have about a finger's width of space at the fingertips.

Can I wash Rockbros cycling gloves in a washing machine?

Most Rockbros cycling gloves can be hand-washed in lukewarm water with mild soap. While some models may be machine-washable, hand-washing is recommended to extend the lifespan of the grip materials and padding.

Yes, the padded palms and reinforced construction provide impact protection during falls. While no glove can prevent all injuries, Rockbros' design minimizes scrapes and reduces hand trauma during accidents.
