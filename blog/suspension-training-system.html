Frequently Asked Questions

What muscles do suspension trainers work?

Suspension trainers work virtually every muscle group including chest, back, shoulders, arms, core, legs, and glutes. They're excellent for functional, full-body conditioning.

Are suspension trainers suitable for beginners?

Yes, suspension trainers are beginner-friendly because you control the difficulty by adjusting your body angle. Start with easier variations and progress as you gain strength and stability.

Can suspension trainers replace dumbbells and weights?

While suspension trainers offer excellent resistance training, combining them with dumbbells and resistance bands creates a more comprehensive home gym for varied training stimulus.

How do I set up a suspension trainer at home?

Most suspension trainers anchor to a door frame, pull-up bar, or sturdy beam. Ensure the anchor point is secure and rated for your body weight before starting workouts.
