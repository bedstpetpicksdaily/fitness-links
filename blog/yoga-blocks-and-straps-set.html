Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between cork and foam yoga blocks?

Cork yoga blocks are firmer, more durable, and eco-friendly but heavier. Foam blocks are lighter, cushioned, and budget-friendly but wear faster with regular use.

Can yoga blocks and straps replace other home gym equipment?

No, yoga blocks and straps are complementary accessories. They work best alongside dumbbells, resistance bands, yoga mats, and other home gym equipment for comprehensive fitness training.

How do yoga straps improve my workout?

Yoga straps extend your reach, deepen stretches safely, and provide support during challenging poses. They're especially beneficial for improving flexibility and preventing injury.

What size yoga blocks should I choose?

Standard yoga blocks measure 4x6x9 inches. Choose based on your height and flexibility level—taller individuals may prefer larger blocks for better support.
