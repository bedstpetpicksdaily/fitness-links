Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Rogue Matador pull-up bar easy to install?

Yes, the Matador requires no tools or permanent installation. It simply wedges into your doorway frame and can be removed instantly without leaving marks or damage.

What is the weight capacity of the Rogue Matador?

The Rogue Matador has a weight capacity of 300+ pounds, making it suitable for most users including heavy athletes and those performing weighted pull-ups.

Can the Matador fit all doorway sizes?

The Matador fits standard doorways with frame widths between 24-36 inches. Always measure your doorway before purchasing to ensure proper fit.

Does the pull-up bar damage door frames?

When installed correctly with protective padding, the Matador should not damage most door frames. However, hollow interior doors and delicate trim may require extra caution.
