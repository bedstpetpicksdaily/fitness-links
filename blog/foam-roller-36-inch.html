Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a 36 inch foam roller?

Use your foam roller 2-3 times per week for 1-2 minutes per muscle group. Daily use is fine for light rolling, but avoid aggressive rolling on the same areas more than once daily.

Is a 36 inch foam roller better than shorter models?

A 36 inch foam roller provides better stability and coverage for larger muscle groups. Shorter rollers offer more portability, so choose based on your available space and fitness goals.

Can I use a foam roller with other equipment like resistance bands?

Absolutely! Foam rollers work great alongside dumbbells, resistance bands, and yoga mats for a complete home gym recovery routine.

What density foam roller should I choose?

Beginners should start with medium-density foam rollers. Advanced users can progress to high-density options for deeper muscle penetration and more intense massage.
