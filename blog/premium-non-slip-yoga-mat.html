Frequently Asked Questions

What thickness is best for a non-slip yoga mat?

Most experts recommend 3-6mm thickness. Standard 3-4mm mats offer portability, while 6mm mats provide maximum cushioning for joint protection during longer sessions.

How do I maintain grip on my non-slip yoga mat?

Clean your mat regularly with mild soap and water, let it air dry completely, and avoid using oils or lotions before practice. A towel or yoga towel can boost grip during sweaty sessions.

Are non-slip yoga mats suitable for all types of exercise?

Yes, non-slip yoga mats work well for yoga, Pilates, stretching, and light home gym exercises. They're particularly beneficial for high-intensity workouts where stability is crucial.

How long do premium non-slip yoga mats last?

High-quality non-slip mats typically last 1-2 years with regular use and proper care. Premium brands often last longer due to superior materials and construction quality.
