Your personal comfort during hot weather riding depends heavily on the tricycle's design and materials. Look for e-trikes with ergonomic seating that promotes airflow and reduces heat buildup. Extended exposure to intense sun poses health risks for seniors and casual riders alike. When selecting your e-trike, choose models with larger battery capacities that accommodate this reduction while still meeting your typical ride distance.
