Frequently Asked Questions

Are cork yoga blocks better than foam blocks?

Cork blocks offer superior grip, durability, and stability compared to foam alternatives. While more expensive, they last longer and provide better support for serious fitness training in home gyms.

Can yoga blocks be used for strength training?

Yes, cork yoga blocks are highly versatile and work excellently for strength training, flexibility work, balance exercises, and as support tools alongside dumbbells and resistance bands.

How do I clean cork yoga blocks?

Simply wipe cork blocks with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid soaking them in water. Allow them to air dry completely before storing with your other home gym equipment.

What size yoga block set is best for home gyms?

Standard 9x6x4 inch cork blocks work best for most home gym setups. A set of 2-3 blocks gives you versatility for various exercises and fitness routines.
