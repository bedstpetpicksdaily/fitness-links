Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between cork and foam yoga blocks?

Cork blocks are denser, more durable, and eco-friendly, offering superior firmness for advanced practitioners. Foam blocks are lighter, more affordable, and better for beginners, though they compress over time with regular use.

How many yoga blocks do I need for my home gym?

Most practitioners benefit from a set of two blocks for balanced support during poses. A set of two allows you to use one block per hand or create stable foundations for deeper stretches.

Can yoga blocks be used with other home gym equipment?

Absolutely. Yoga blocks pair perfectly with yoga mats for cushioning, resistance bands for modified stretches, and dumbbells for strength training modifications and stability work.

Are yoga blocks suitable for beginners?

Yes, yoga blocks are ideal for beginners as they help achieve proper alignment, reduce strain, and allow safe modifications of challenging poses while building strength and flexibility.
