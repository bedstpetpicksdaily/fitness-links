Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between cork and foam yoga blocks?

Cork blocks are denser, more durable, and provide better stability for intense practices, while foam blocks are lighter, cushioned, and more affordable for beginners. Choose based on your budget and practice intensity.

Can yoga straps help with flexibility training?

Yes, yoga straps allow you to safely extend stretches and hold poses longer without forcing your body, gradually improving flexibility over time.

Are yoga blocks necessary for home workouts?

While not strictly necessary, blocks significantly enhance your practice by improving alignment, preventing injury, and making advanced poses accessible to all fitness levels.

How do yoga blocks complement other home gym equipment?

Yoga blocks work well alongside resistance bands for stretching, yoga mats for cushioning, and dumbbells for strength training to create a balanced home fitness routine.
