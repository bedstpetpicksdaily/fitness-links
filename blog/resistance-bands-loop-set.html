Frequently Asked Questions

What resistance levels should a beginner look for?

Beginners should start with lighter resistance bands (typically light to medium) and progress gradually. Most quality sets include multiple resistance levels, allowing you to build strength over time.

How do resistance bands compare to dumbbells?

Resistance bands offer variable resistance that increases as you stretch them, while dumbbells provide constant weight. Bands are more portable and joint-friendly, making them ideal for home gyms and rehabilitation.

Can I use resistance bands for full-body workouts?

Yes, resistance bands are incredibly versatile and can target every major muscle group including legs, glutes, chest, back, shoulders, and arms with proper exercise selection.

How long do resistance band loop sets typically last?

Quality resistance bands can last 1-2 years with regular use. Durability depends on material quality, usage intensity, and proper storage away from direct sunlight.
