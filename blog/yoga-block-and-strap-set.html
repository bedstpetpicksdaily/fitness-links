Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between cork and foam yoga blocks?

Cork blocks offer superior durability and grip, making them ideal for long-term use. Foam blocks are lighter and more affordable, better suited for beginners or travel.

Can yoga straps be used for resistance training?

Yes, yoga straps work well for assisted stretching and can provide light resistance. However, for serious resistance training, dedicated resistance bands offer better support.

Are yoga blocks necessary for beginners?

While not strictly necessary, yoga blocks significantly improve form and safety for beginners by providing support during foundational poses and stretches.

How do I choose the right yoga block height?

Standard blocks are 3 inches thick. Choose based on your flexibility level—beginners may prefer higher blocks for easier support, while advanced users often use standard heights.
