Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Boogie Bounce rebounders and regular trampolines?

Boogie Bounce rebounders are specifically engineered for fitness with optimized bounce tension, smaller diameters (typically 38-40 inches), and safety features designed for controlled cardio workouts. Regular trampolines are larger and designed primarily for recreational jumping, not fitness training.

Is bounce equipment safe for children?

Yes, when used properly with appropriate supervision. Boogie Bounce mini trampolines include safety padding, non-slip surfaces, and stability features. Always follow age and weight recommendations, supervise younger children, and start with low-intensity bouncing techniques.

How many calories can you burn bouncing for 30 minutes?

Most people burn 200-300 calories during 30 minutes of moderate bouncing on a rebounder, depending on body weight and intensity. Boogie Bounce equipment's design optimizes energy efficiency, making every bounce count toward your fitness goals.

Can bounce equipment help with balance and coordination?

Absolutely. Bouncing naturally improves proprioception, core engagement, and balance. Boogie Bounce rebounders with stability bars are particularly effective for developing coordination while providing security for beginners or older users.

What size Boogie Bounce rebounder should I choose for a family?

For family use, 40-inch rebounders offer the best balance of safety, bounce quality, and space efficiency. Check Boogie Bounce's weight capacity ratings to ensure the equipment supports all family members' combined use.
