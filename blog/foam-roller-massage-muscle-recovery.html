Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a foam roller?

Most fitness experts recommend foam rolling 1-2 times daily for 30-60 seconds per muscle group. Use it post-workout for recovery or before workouts to activate muscles.

Can I use a foam roller if I have an injury?

Avoid foam rolling directly on injured areas. Consult your physical therapist or doctor before using a foam roller if you have acute injuries or chronic pain conditions.

What's the difference between smooth and textured foam rollers?

Smooth rollers provide gentle, broad pressure for general recovery. Textured or ridged rollers offer deeper, more targeted deep tissue massage for intensive muscle work.

Do vibrating foam rollers work better?

Vibrating foam rollers can enhance blood flow and reduce muscle tension faster than standard rollers. They're beneficial but more expensive—choose based on your budget and recovery needs.
