Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between foam and cork yoga blocks?

Foam blocks are lightweight, cushioned, and affordable, making them ideal for beginners. Cork blocks are firmer, more durable, and provide better support for advanced practitioners. Choose based on your skill level and budget.

How many blocks do I need for my home gym?

Most practitioners benefit from having two blocks for balanced support during poses. A complete set typically includes two blocks, which is perfect for home gym setups.

Can yoga blocks work with other home gym equipment?

Absolutely. Yoga blocks complement resistance bands, dumbbells, and yoga mats perfectly. Use them for stability during dumbbell work or as props alongside your other fitness accessories.

Are yoga blocks suitable for beginners?

Yes, blocks are especially beneficial for beginners. They provide necessary support, help with proper alignment, and make challenging poses more accessible as you build strength and flexibility.
