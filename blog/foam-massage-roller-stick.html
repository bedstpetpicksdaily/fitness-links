Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a massage roller stick?

Use a massage roller stick 2-3 times per week for 30-60 seconds per muscle group. Daily use is safe but not necessary for most users.

Can I use a massage roller stick before or after workouts?

Use it after workouts for recovery, or before as a warm-up tool. Post-workout use is more beneficial for muscle soreness relief.

What muscles can I target with a roller stick?

Target calves, quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, back, shoulders, and arms. Avoid rolling directly on joints or bones.

Is a massage roller stick worth buying for home gyms?

Yes, it's an affordable recovery essential that complements dumbbells and resistance bands while improving flexibility and reducing soreness.
