Frequently Asked Questions

Can you really walk on a treadmill while working?

Yes, most people adapt quickly to walking on an under desk pad while typing or attending meetings. Start at a comfortable pace and gradually increase as you adjust.

What's the weight capacity of most walking pads?

Most quality under desk walking pads support between 220-300 pounds. Always check individual product specifications before purchasing.

How much space do under desk treadmills require?

Compact walking pads typically measure 48-55 inches long and 28-30 inches wide, fitting easily under most standing desks.

Are walking pads as effective as traditional treadmills?

Walking pads excel at increasing daily activity and low-impact exercise. For high-intensity cardio training, traditional treadmills offer more features.
