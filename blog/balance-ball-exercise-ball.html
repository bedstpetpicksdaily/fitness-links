Frequently Asked Questions

What size exercise ball should I choose?

Ball size depends on your height. Generally, 55cm balls suit users 5'1"-5'7", 65cm for 5'8"-6'0", and 75cm for those over 6'1". Choose the size that allows your knees to bend at 90 degrees when seated.

Are exercise balls suitable for beginners?

Yes, exercise balls are beginner-friendly. Start with basic seated exercises and gradually progress to more challenging movements as your stability and core strength improve.

How does an exercise ball compare to yoga mats and resistance bands?

Exercise balls provide instability training that engages stabilizer muscles, while yoga mats offer support for floor exercises and resistance bands provide variable resistance. Together, they create a well-rounded home gym.

How often should I replace my exercise ball?

With proper care and regular maintenance, most exercise balls last 1-2 years. Replace it if it loses air retention, develops cracks, or no longer holds proper inflation.
