Are FED Fitness dumbbells suitable for beginners?

Yes, absolutely. FED Fitness offers dumbbells in various weight ranges, including lighter options perfect for beginners. Their adjustable dumbbell systems are particularly ideal for new lifters who want flexibility as they progress and build strength.

What's the difference between adjustable and fixed-weight dumbbells?

Adjustable dumbbells allow you to change weights by adding or removing plates, saving space and money. Fixed-weight dumbbells are ready to use immediately and don't require adjustment, making them faster for circuit training and group workouts.

How much space do I need for a dumbbell set?

Space requirements depend on your setup. Adjustable dumbbells from FED Fitness are compact and perfect for small spaces. Fixed-weight sets require a dumbbell rack, which can be wall-mounted or placed in a corner. FED Fitness offers various storage solutions.
