Frequently Asked Questions

What resistance levels should beginners choose?

Beginners typically benefit from lighter resistance bands (light to medium). A complete set with multiple resistance levels allows you to progress safely and adjust difficulty as your strength improves.

How do resistance bands compare to dumbbells?

Resistance bands offer variable resistance and are more portable than dumbbells, making them ideal for home gyms. Dumbbells provide fixed weight resistance. Many fitness enthusiasts use both for comprehensive training.

Can resistance bands replace a complete home gym?

Resistance bands are excellent for strength training but work best as part of a complete home gym setup that may include yoga mats, treadmills, or adjustable dumbbells for well-rounded fitness.

How long do quality resistance bands last?

High-quality resistance band sets can last 1-2+ years with proper care. Store them away from direct sunlight and extreme heat, and avoid overstretching to maximize durability.
