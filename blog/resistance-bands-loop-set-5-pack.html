Frequently Asked Questions

What resistance levels are included in the 5-pack set?

Most 5-pack resistance band sets include light, medium, heavy, and extra-heavy resistance levels, allowing progressive training as strength increases. Check product specifications for exact resistance amounts in pounds.

Can resistance bands replace dumbbells for strength training?

Resistance bands provide excellent strength training benefits and can complement or partially replace dumbbells. They offer unique advantages like variable resistance and are ideal for home gyms with limited space.

How do I maintain and store resistance loop bands?

Store bands away from direct sunlight and heat in a cool, dry place. Keep them separate to prevent sticking, and avoid contact with sharp objects. Proper care extends their lifespan significantly.

Are resistance bands suitable for physical therapy and rehabilitation?

Yes, resistance bands are commonly recommended by physical therapists for rehabilitation exercises. Their adjustable resistance and low impact make them ideal for recovery and injury prevention programs.
