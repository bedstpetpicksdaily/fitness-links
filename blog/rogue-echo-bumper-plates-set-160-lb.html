Do Rogue Echo Bumper Plates really reduce noise, or is that marketing hype?

No hype here. The engineered rubber construction genuinely absorbs impact. Multiple verified buyers report their home gyms are now usable before 7 AM without disturbing roommates or neighbors. You'll still hear some sound on drop, but it's muffled rather than a sharp metallic crash.

Will 160 LB be enough weight for my home gym long-term?

It depends on your strength level and goals. If you're squatting or deadlifting under 300 LB for reps, this set works fine.
