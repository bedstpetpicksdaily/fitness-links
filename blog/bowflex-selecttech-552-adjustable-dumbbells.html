Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum weight of the Bowflex SelectTech 552?

The maximum weight is 52.5 pounds per dumbbell, making them suitable for intermediate strength training and progressive overload workouts.

How much space do these adjustable dumbbells require?

They require minimal space compared to traditional dumbbells. A pair takes up roughly the size of one standard dumbbell rack, making them perfect for small home gyms and apartments.

Are the SelectTech 552 dumbbells easy to adjust between weights?

Yes, the dial-select mechanism allows you to change weights in seconds without picking up multiple dumbbells, streamlining your workout sessions.

Can I use these dumbbells for heavy compound exercises?

The SelectTech 552 works well for most compound exercises like dumbbell presses, rows, and curls. However, advanced lifters may eventually outgrow the 52.5-pound maximum weight.
