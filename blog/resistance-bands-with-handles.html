Frequently Asked Questions

What resistance level should beginners choose?

Beginners should start with light to medium resistance bands (typically 5-15 lbs) to master proper form before progressing to heavier resistance levels.

Are resistance bands with handles safe for strength training?

Yes, when used correctly with proper form and appropriate resistance levels, resistance bands are safe and effective for building strength and muscle endurance.

How long do resistance bands typically last?

Quality resistance bands can last 1-3 years with proper care. Storing them away from direct sunlight and heat extends their lifespan significantly.

Can resistance bands replace dumbbells in a home gym?

Resistance bands are excellent complementary tools and work well for many exercises, though many fitness experts recommend combining them with dumbbells for optimal results.
