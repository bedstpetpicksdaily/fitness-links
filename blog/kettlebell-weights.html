Frequently Asked Questions

What weight kettlebell should a beginner start with?

Beginners typically start with 8-12 kg (18-26 lbs) kettlebells for women and 12-16 kg (26-35 lbs) for men. This allows proper form development before progressing to heavier weights.

Are cast iron kettlebells better than adjustable ones?

Cast iron kettlebells are durable and traditional, while adjustable kettlebells save space. Choose cast iron for superior durability and authentic feel, or adjustable for convenience and variety.

How much space do kettlebells require in a home gym?

Kettlebells are extremely space-efficient. A small collection requires only 2-3 square feet of floor space, making them ideal for apartments and compact home gyms.

Can kettlebells replace dumbbells in a home gym?

Kettlebells offer different benefits than dumbbells with dynamic swinging movements. Ideally, having both provides the most complete home gym, but kettlebells alone deliver excellent full-body training.
