Frequently Asked Questions

Are doorway pull-up bars safe for all door frames?

Most doorway pull-up bars are safe on standard wooden door frames, but avoid installation on hollow doors or weak frames. Always check weight capacity and follow manufacturer instructions carefully.

What weight capacity should I look for?

Look for bars supporting at least 250-300 lbs for general use. Heavier users or those planning advanced movements should choose models rated for 400+ lbs.

Can doorway pull-up bars damage my door frame?

With proper installation and appropriate weight, damage is minimal. Use padded grips to reduce pressure points and avoid exceeding weight limits to protect your door frame.

What exercises can I do with a doorway pull-up bar?

Beyond pull-ups and chin-ups, you can perform hanging leg raises, assisted pull-ups using resistance bands, and dead hangs for decompression and grip strength.
