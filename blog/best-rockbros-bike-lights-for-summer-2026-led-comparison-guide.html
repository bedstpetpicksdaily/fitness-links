Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Rockbros bike light batteries last on a single charge?

Battery life varies by model and brightness mode, typically ranging from 4-20 hours. Front lights on maximum brightness last 4-8 hours, while lower settings extend runtime to 15-20 hours. Rockbros publishes specific runtime charts for each model on their product pages.

Are Rockbros bike lights waterproof for all weather conditions?

Most Rockbros lights feature IPX6 waterproof ratings, protecting against rain and splashes. However, they're not designed for submersion. This rating ensures reliable performance in typical summer weather, including thunderstorms and wet conditions.

Can Rockbros bike lights mount on different bike types?

Yes, Rockbros lights come with multiple mounting options compatible with road bikes, mountain bikes, and hybrid cycles. Their handlebar mounts and seat post clamps adjust to various diameters, and they offer alternative attachment methods for unconventional frame designs.

What makes Rockbros lights better than other cycling brands?

Rockbros combines superior LED technology, durable construction, competitive pricing, and excellent customer support. Their lights integrate seamlessly with other Rockbros cycling accessories, creating a cohesive gear ecosystem for cyclists.

Do Rockbros bike lights include rechargeable batteries?

Most current Rockbros models feature built-in USB rechargeable batteries. Some entry-level options use disposable batteries, but the brand's primary focus is on rechargeable solutions for convenience and sustainability.
