Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a foam roller?

Most fitness experts recommend foam rolling 1-2 minutes per muscle group, 3-5 times per week for optimal results. Daily use is safe for recovery purposes.

Can beginners use foam rollers?

Yes, beginners can use foam rollers but should start slowly with lighter pressure. Begin with softer rollers and gradually progress to firmer options as your muscles adapt.

What's the difference between foam rollers and massage guns?

Foam rollers use sustained pressure and manual rolling for self-myofascial release, while massage guns use rapid vibrations. Both are effective but offer different recovery experiences.

Should I foam roll before or after workouts?

Light foam rolling before workouts can improve mobility, while post-workout foam rolling (within 48 hours) is more effective for reducing soreness and accelerating recovery.
