Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment is essential to start a home gym?

The essentials include adjustable dumbbells, a weight bench, and either a barbell with plates or a power rack. These items provide a solid foundation for most strength training exercises.

How much space do I need for a home gym?

A basic home gym can fit in 100-150 square feet. If space is limited, focus on compact equipment like dumbbells and power towers. FED Fitness offers space-efficient solutions for smaller areas.

Is FED Fitness equipment suitable for beginners?

Yes, FED Fitness offers equipment for all fitness levels. Their adjustable dumbbells, benches, and machines are perfect for beginners while scaling up for advanced lifters.

How do I maintain my home gym equipment?

Keep equipment clean and dry, regularly inspect for wear, store weights properly, and perform routine maintenance on machines. Quality FED Fitness equipment requires minimal maintenance due to its durability.

Can I build a complete gym gradually?

Absolutely. Start with dumbbells and a bench, then add a barbell, power rack, and cardio equipment as your budget allows. FED Fitness products integrate seamlessly into growing gym setups.
