Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a yoga mat non-slip?

Non-slip yoga mats feature textured rubber or PVC surfaces with superior grip properties. The raised pattern increases friction between your skin and the mat, preventing your hands and feet from sliding during poses and exercises.

What thickness should I choose for a yoga mat?

Standard yoga mats range from 1/8 inch to 1/4 inch thick. Thicker mats (5-6mm) provide more cushioning and joint protection, while thinner mats (3mm) offer better stability and portability.

Are non-slip yoga mats good for other exercises?

Yes, non-slip yoga mats work great for Pilates, stretching, bodyweight exercises, and floor-based workouts. They're a versatile home gym accessory that complements dumbbells, resistance bands, and other fitness equipment.

How do I clean and maintain a non-slip yoga mat?

Wipe your mat with a damp cloth after each use to remove sweat and bacteria. For deeper cleaning, use mild soap and water or a yoga mat cleaner spray. Allow it to air dry completely before rolling it up for storage.
