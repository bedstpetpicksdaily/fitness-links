Frequently Asked Questions

What thickness should a yoga mat be?

6mm is considered the sweet spot for most practitioners. It provides adequate cushioning for joints while maintaining balance stability during standing poses.

How do I prevent my yoga mat from slipping?

Use a non-slip mat like those reviewed here, place it on a carpet or yoga towel, or apply a grip-enhancing mat spray for added traction.

How often should I replace my yoga mat?

With regular use, a quality non-slip mat typically lasts 1-2 years. Replace it sooner if you notice decreased grip, cracks, or visible wear.

Are 6mm yoga mats suitable for all types of exercise?

Yes, 6mm mats work well for yoga, pilates, Pilates, stretching, and light cardio. For high-impact workouts, consider thicker gym mats or flooring.
