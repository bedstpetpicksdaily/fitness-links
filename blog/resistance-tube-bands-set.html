Frequently Asked Questions

What resistance levels do tube bands sets typically include?

Most quality resistance tube bands sets include 4-6 different resistance levels, usually color-coded from light (yellow/red) to heavy (black/silver). This allows progressive overload as your strength improves.

Are resistance tube bands better than dumbbells for home gyms?

Both have advantages. Resistance bands are more affordable, portable, and space-efficient, while dumbbells provide consistent resistance and are better for heavy strength training. Many experts recommend having both for a complete home gym.

How long do resistance tube bands typically last?

High-quality resistance tube bands can last 1-2 years with proper care and regular use. Store them away from direct sunlight, avoid extreme temperatures, and prevent overstretching to extend their lifespan.

Can beginners use resistance tube bands safely?

Yes, resistance tube bands are very beginner-friendly. Start with lighter resistance levels, focus on proper form, and gradually progress. They're also excellent for rehabilitation and physical therapy exercises.
