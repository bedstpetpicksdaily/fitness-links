Rebounders have exploded in popularity over the past few years, and for good reason. Summer is the ideal season to invest in one since you can use it both indoors during hot afternoons and outdoors in the early morning or evening hours.

How long does a typical rebounder workout take?

Effective rebounder sessions typically range from 15-30 minutes. Beginners should start with 10-15 minute sessions and gradually increase duration as balance and fitness improve.
