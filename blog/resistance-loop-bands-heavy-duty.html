Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between fabric and latex resistance bands?

Fabric resistance bands offer better comfort and grip, while latex provides higher durability. Fabric bands are ideal for glute work, whereas latex suits general strength training.

How long do heavy duty resistance bands last?

Quality heavy duty bands typically last 1-3 years with regular use. Proper storage and avoiding sharp objects extends their lifespan significantly.

Can resistance bands replace dumbbells?

Resistance bands and dumbbells offer different benefits. Bands provide constant tension, while dumbbells offer progressive weight. Combining both creates the best home gym setup.

Are resistance loop bands good for beginners?

Yes, resistance bands are excellent for beginners. They're safer than heavy weights, easier to control, and allow you to build strength gradually before progressing.
