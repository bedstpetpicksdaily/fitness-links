Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a foam roller?

For best results, use your foam roller 3-5 times per week for 1-2 minutes per muscle group. Daily use is safe but may not provide additional benefits beyond consistent 3-5 day routines.

Can foam rolling prevent injuries?

Yes, regular foam rolling improves tissue quality, flexibility, and circulation, which can reduce injury risk when combined with proper form and progressive training in your home gym.

What density foam roller should I buy?

Beginners should start with low to medium-density rollers for comfort. Advanced users can progress to high-density rollers for deeper tissue work and intense muscle activation.

Is foam rolling better than stretching?

Foam rolling and stretching serve different purposes. Foam rolling releases muscle tension and improves circulation, while stretching enhances flexibility. Use both for comprehensive recovery.
