Frequently Asked Questions

What weight kettlebell should I start with?

Beginners should typically start with 8-12 kg (18-26 lbs) for women and 16-20 kg (35-44 lbs) for men. Start light to master proper form before progressing to heavier weights.

Can kettlebells replace dumbbells in a home gym?

Kettlebells and dumbbells offer different benefits. Kettlebells excel at dynamic, functional movements, while dumbbells are better for isolation exercises. Ideally, use both for a complete home gym setup.

How do I choose between cast iron and vinyl kettlebells?

Cast iron kettlebells are more durable and affordable long-term, while vinyl-coated kettlebells protect floors and are quieter. Choose vinyl if you have sensitive flooring; cast iron for durability.

What kettlebell exercises burn the most calories?

Kettlebell swings, snatches, and Turkish get-ups burn the most calories due to their explosive, full-body nature. These movements engage multiple muscle groups simultaneously.
