Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a yoga mat non-slip?

Non-slip yoga mats feature textured surfaces made from materials like natural rubber, TPE, or microfiber that create friction between the mat and floor, preventing sliding during exercise.

Is 6mm thickness good for yoga?

Yes, 6mm thickness is ideal for yoga as it provides adequate cushioning for joints while maintaining stability and balance during poses without excessive bulk.

Can I use a yoga mat for other home gym exercises?

Absolutely! Non-slip yoga mats work great for pilates, stretching, core work, and floor exercises, making them a versatile addition to your home fitness equipment.

How do I maintain my non-slip yoga mat?

Regular cleaning with mild soap and water helps maintain the non-slip surface. Allow it to air dry completely before rolling it up for storage.
