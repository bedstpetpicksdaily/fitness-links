Frequently Asked Questions

Are resistance band handles compatible with all bands?

Most handles fit standard resistance bands, but verify compatibility. Check the handle's slot width matches your band thickness for secure attachment.

How do I attach resistance band handles?

Simply slide the resistance band through the handle slots. Some handles feature door anchors that loop over doors for anchored exercises and stability.

What exercises can I do with band handles?

Bicep curls, tricep extensions, chest presses, rows, lateral raises, and shoulder exercises are popular. Handles provide better control than bare bands for targeted training.

How durable are resistance band handles?

Quality handles last years with regular use. Look for non-slip grip materials, reinforced stitching, and sturdy construction to ensure long-term durability.
