Frequently Asked Questions

What thickness knee sleeves should I choose for home workouts?

For home gym training, 5mm sleeves work well for moderate intensity, while 7mm provides extra support for heavy lifting and competitive training.

How do I know if knee sleeves fit properly?

Proper fit should feel snug without restricting circulation. The sleeve should stay in place during movement and provide noticeable compression without discomfort.

Can knee sleeves prevent knee injuries?

While sleeves provide compression and stability support, they work best combined with proper form, adequate warm-ups, and progressive training to minimize injury risk.

Are knee sleeves necessary for home gym training?

Knee sleeves aren't mandatory but are highly beneficial for intense strength training, heavy squats, and leg exercises, especially for those with joint concerns.
