Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a foam roller?

Most fitness experts recommend foam rolling 3-5 times per week for 1-2 minutes per muscle group. You can use it post-workout or on rest days for recovery and mobility work.

Is foam rolling safe for everyone?

Foam rolling is generally safe, but people with injuries, chronic conditions, or extreme muscle soreness should consult a healthcare provider first. Avoid rolling directly on joints or bones.

What's the difference between foam roller densities?

Soft rollers are better for beginners and sensitive muscles, while medium and high-density rollers provide deeper massage for advanced users and harder-to-reach muscle groups.

Can foam rolling help with muscle tightness?

Yes, foam rolling breaks up muscle knots, reduces tension, and improves blood flow to tight areas, making it excellent for addressing muscle tightness from dumbbells, resistance training, or cardio.
