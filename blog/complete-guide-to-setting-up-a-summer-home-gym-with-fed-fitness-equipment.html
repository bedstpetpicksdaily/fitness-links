Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum space needed for a summer home gym?

You can start with as little as 100-150 square feet. A basic setup with FED Fitness adjustable dumbbells and a weight bench requires minimal space. As you expand with barbells, racks, and cardio machines, aim for 200-400 square feet for comfortable, safe training.

Are FED Fitness equipment suitable for outdoor use?

Yes, FED Fitness equipment is designed with durable materials that withstand outdoor conditions. However, we recommend protective covers for extended outdoor storage and regular maintenance to ensure longevity in summer heat and weather.

How do I choose between fixed and adjustable dumbbells from FED Fitness?

Adjustable dumbbells save space and money, making them ideal for home gyms with limited room. Fixed dumbbells from FED Fitness are better if you have adequate space and prefer traditional training. Both options deliver excellent results.

Do I need a power rack for a home gym?

While not essential, a FED Fitness power rack significantly enhances safety for heavy lifting and enables you to train without a spotter. It's highly recommended if you plan to perform compound lifts like squats, bench presses, and deadlifts.

What should I prioritize when buying FED Fitness equipment?

Start with versatile pieces: adjustable dumbbells, a quality weight bench, and barbells with plates. These form the foundation of effective training. Add power racks, power towers, and cardio machines as your budget and space allow.
