What is the difference between a rebounder and a mini trampoline?

Rebounders typically feature firmer bounce surfaces and are optimized for cardio training and fitness workouts. Mini trampolines offer softer bouncing and are often used for balance training and recreational exercise. Boogie Bounce offers both types to suit different fitness preferences.

Yes, Boogie Bounce products are designed with beginner safety in mind. Their low-impact bounce action reduces joint stress, and many models include stability bars. Always start with shorter sessions and proper form to build confidence.
