Frequently Asked Questions

How does Hydrow Wave compare to Concept2 Model D?

Concept2 wins on durability and resale value—it's the standard for serious rowers. Hydrow Wave wins on coaching, app integration, and entertainment factor. Concept2 feels like buying a hammer; Hydrow feels like joining a gym. Pick based on whether community matters more than pure simplicity.

Can you actually get a full-body workout from a rowing machine?

Absolutely, but context matters. Rowing engages roughly 85% of your muscles during the drive phase, but it's not a replacement for upper body strength training. Pair it with resistance bands or dumbbells for comprehensive fitness. The Wave's classes emphasize compound movements, which helps.

Is the $2,000+ price worth it versus $300 budget rowers?

Budget rowers use cheap hydraulics that degrade within months—I've seen people abandon them after 3 weeks. Hydrow's magnetic resistance stays consistent for years. Plus durability compounds savings. However, if you're testing whether you'll actually row, start cheaper and upgrade after proving commitment.
