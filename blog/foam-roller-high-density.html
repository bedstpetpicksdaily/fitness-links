Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a high density foam roller?

For optimal results, use your foam roller 2-3 times per week for 1-2 minutes per muscle group. Beginners should start with lighter pressure and gradually increase intensity as muscles adapt.

Is high density foam roller better than standard density?

High density foam rollers provide deeper muscle penetration and are more durable for frequent use. They're ideal for serious athletes, while standard density works better for beginners and recovery-focused users.

Can foam rolling help with muscle soreness?

Yes, foam rolling effectively reduces delayed onset muscle soreness (DOMS) by improving blood flow and breaking up muscle tension. Use it 24-48 hours after intense workouts for best results.

What size foam roller should I buy?

Standard 36-inch foam rollers work for most users and muscle groups. Compact 12-18 inch rollers are portable options, while 24-inch sizes offer good balance between portability and surface area.
