Frequently Asked Questions

What weight range do the Bowflex SelectTech 560 dumbbells offer?

The SelectTech 560 adjustable dumbbells range from 5 to 80 pounds per dumbbell, providing 15 different weight settings. This wide range makes them suitable for beginners through advanced lifters.

Do I need the app to use these dumbbells?

No, the Bowflex SelectTech 560 works perfectly without the app. The dial-select mechanism is mechanical and requires no batteries or connectivity. The app is optional and adds workout tracking features.

How much space do adjustable dumbbells save?

One pair of SelectTech 560 dumbbells replaces 30 traditional dumbbells, saving approximately 80% of the space compared to a full dumbbell rack set.

Are these dumbbells good for strength training?

Yes, with weights up to 80 pounds per dumbbell, the SelectTech 560 is excellent for strength training, muscle building, and progressive resistance workouts for most fitness levels.

How durable are Bowflex SelectTech 560 dumbbells?

These dumbbells feature a durable composite shell and metal frame designed for long-term use. Most users report years of reliable performance with proper care and maintenance.
