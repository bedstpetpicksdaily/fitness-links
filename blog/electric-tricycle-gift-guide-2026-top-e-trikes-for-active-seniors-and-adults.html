Frequently Asked Questions

Are electric tricycles safe for seniors?

Yes, e-trikes are specifically designed with safety in mind. The three-wheel design provides superior stability compared to bikes, and quality models like Mooncool feature responsive brakes, LED lighting, and ergonomic seating with back support. Always ensure the gift recipient wears a helmet and practices safe riding habits.

How far can a Mooncool electric tricycle travel on a single charge?

Most Mooncool e-trike models offer a range of 30-50 miles per charge, depending on battery capacity, terrain, and assist level used. This makes them perfect for recreational rides and neighborhood exploration without frequent recharging.

What's the weight capacity of typical adult electric tricycles?

Most quality e-trikes, including Mooncool models, support riders weighing 250-350+ pounds. Check specific product specifications to confirm weight capacity matches your gift recipient's needs.

Do I need a license to ride an electric tricycle?

In most U.S. states, e-trikes classified as Class 1 or Class 2 (with motors under 750W and speeds under 20-28 mph) don't require licenses or registration. However, regulations vary by location—check local laws for your area.

How long do e-trike batteries typically last?

Quality lithium-ion batteries used in Mooncool electric tricycles usually last 3-5 years or 500-1000 charge cycles. Proper maintenance and storage practices can extend battery lifespan significantly.
