Frequently Asked Questions

What is TPE material and why is it better than PVC?

TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) is an eco-friendly, non-toxic material that offers superior grip and durability compared to PVC. It's naturally non-slip, hypoallergenic, and better for the environment, making it the preferred choice for health-conscious fitness enthusiasts.

Is a 6mm yoga mat thick enough for cushioning?

Yes, 6mm thickness is the ideal balance between cushioning and stability. It provides adequate joint protection for yoga, pilates, and floor exercises while maintaining proper alignment and balance without excessive softness.

How do I maintain my non-slip yoga mat?

Clean your mat regularly with mild soap and water, then air dry completely. Avoid excessive moisture and store it rolled up in a cool, dry place. Regular maintenance helps preserve the non-slip surface and extends the mat's lifespan.

Can I use this mat for high-intensity workouts?

Absolutely! The non-slip surface and 6mm cushioning make it perfect for yoga, pilates, HIIT, core training, and stretching exercises. The grippy TPE material prevents slipping even during intense, sweaty workouts.
