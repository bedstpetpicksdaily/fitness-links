Assess Your Space and Create a Layout

Before purchasing any equipment, measure your available outdoor space carefully. Consider factors like ground surface, weather exposure, and accessibility. Most outdoor gyms require a minimum of 10x15 feet, though larger spaces allow for more versatility. FED Fitness equipment is designed to withstand outdoor conditions while maintaining stability and durability. Plan your layout to include zones for strength training, cardio, and functional movements. Ensure adequate spacing between stations to allow for safe movement and proper exercise execution.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much space do I need for an outdoor home gym?

A minimum of 10x15 feet is recommended for basic equipment setup. This allows adequate spacing for safety and proper exercise form. Larger spaces (15x20 feet or more) provide room for additional cardio machines and functional training areas. Consider your equipment choices—a power rack requires more overhead clearance than a weight bench.

Will FED Fitness equipment rust in outdoor conditions?

FED Fitness equipment features rust-resistant coatings and weather-treated materials designed specifically for outdoor use. However, regular maintenance—such as covering equipment during off-seasons and occasional cleaning—extends equipment lifespan significantly. Their power racks and dumbbells are engineered to withstand moisture and UV exposure.

What's the best order to purchase FED Fitness equipment?

Start with adjustable dumbbells and a weight bench for essential strength training. Next, add a barbell and weight plates for compound lifts. Then invest in a power rack to enable safe, progressive barbell training. Finally, incorporate cardio machines and power towers to create a well-rounded facility. This progression allows gradual investment while building functional training capacity.

Can I use FED Fitness equipment year-round outdoors?

Yes, FED Fitness equipment is designed for year-round outdoor use. Their materials resist rust, corrosion, and UV damage. In harsh winter climates, consider protective covers for extended storage periods. Regular maintenance and occasional cleaning keep equipment in optimal condition regardless of season.

How much does a complete FED Fitness outdoor gym cost?

A basic outdoor gym with FED Fitness equipment (dumbbells, bench, barbell, plates, power rack) typically costs $800-$1,500 depending on equipment choices and weight capacities. Premium setups with cardio machines and additional accessories can range from $2,000-$3,500. This investment is recouped through years of training without monthly gym membership fees.
